# Community

Vedetta is an open-source project and we welcome contributors, testers, and security enthusiasts of all levels.

## Discord

Join the Vedetta community on Discord to ask questions, share your setup, report issues, and discuss threat intelligence.

**[Join the Discord](https://discord.gg/aubRTSWRyc)**

Channels include:

- **#announcements** — releases, milestones, and project updates
- **#general** — open discussion
- **#support** — help with installation, configuration, and troubleshooting
- **#bug-reports** — structured bug reporting (forum channel)
- **#feature-requests** — suggest and vote on features (forum channel)
- **#dev-discussion** — architecture, PRs, and implementation talk
- **#threat-intel** — share findings, IOCs, and detection ideas
- **#showcase** — show off your Vedetta deployment

## Contributing

See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for guidelines on submitting pull requests, reporting bugs, and proposing features.

## Code of Conduct

We expect all community members to be respectful and constructive. Harassment, spam, and off-topic self-promotion will result in removal.
